Get the dirt on why we are leaving

Satisfy your thirst now.

It is with mixed feelings that we (Brendan and Sofiya) announce that we will be leaving Vancouver. In the final week of August we will walk out of our tiny apartment for the last time and become traveling nomads. After spending some time visiting family (in Duncan, Toronto, and Bulgaria), we will start with a month in Portugal and figure it out from there. We will travel until we're tired of it and then settle down somewhere (Berlin?) until we're tired of that, too. You can see a more detailed itinerary here: LINK

In the next 3-4 weeks we need to sell, store, or pack all of our belongings. We have a list of items for sale (LINK), please take a look, and please let us know if we might have something else that you want - almost everything we own is for sale. There is also a site that we will use to post where we will be, where we're going, and Sofi may even do some blog posts or something. Brendan won't though, so you'll probably just have to FaceTime us.

This is truly a once in a lifetime opportunity. We are unbelievably fortunate to have the means to be able to support ourselves regardless of location at a time in our lives when we are not tied to any particular place. This opportunity couldn't have happened without the support of our friends, family, and colleagues, and we are so incredibly grateful for all that YOU have done for us. Vancouver will always hold a special place in our heart, and it will always be full of real and adopted family (that's YOU). There are so many thousands of excellent memories here and for that we both extend a big THANK YOU! The last 5-10 years spent in Vancouver feel like a lifetime already [Brendan: That's because we've been dating for five years. Har har.

This is not goodbye but a new chapter in which you get to plan a vacation and meet us across some ocean. Or you can plan a vacation and we'll come meet you ;)
