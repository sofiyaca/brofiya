With mixed feelings, we announce that we will be leaving Vancouver. In the final week of August, we will walk out of our tiny apartment for the last time and become traveling nomads.

After spending some time visiting family in Duncan, Toronto, and Bulgaria, we will start with a month in Portugal and figure it out from there. We will travel until we're tired of it and then settle down, potentially in Berlin, until we're tired of that, too. We've recorded our itinerary so far, and rest assured that we will return to Canada one day.

In the next few weeks, we will be selling, storing or packing all of our belongings. We have a list of items for sale, please take a look, and let us know if you're interested in purchasing any of these items.

If you would like to keep in touch and follow along with our journey, please come back to this website that we will use to post where we're going and other updates. Alternatively, FaceTime us!

This is truly a once in a lifetime opportunity! We are unbelievably fortunate to be able to support ourselves from any location at a time in our lives when our home consists only of easily movable objects and people.

We will miss Vancouver. We will miss the mountains and the adventures and the music and the work. But after much reflection, we've realized what what we will miss most is you. There have been thousands of excellent memories that you all have made with us and we want to thank you all for being a part of it, and a part of us. This opportunity couldn't have happened without the support of our friends, family, and colleagues. We are so incredibly grateful for all that you have done for us. Vancouver will always hold a special place in our hearts, and it will always be full of real and adopted family — that's YOU!

Love,
Sofiya and Brendan
