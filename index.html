With mixed feelings, we announce that we will be leaving Vancouver! In the final week of August, we will walk out of our tiny apartment for the last time and become traveling nomads.

After spending some time visiting family in Duncan, Toronto, and Bulgaria, we will start with a month in Portugal and figure it out from there. We will travel until we're tired of it and then settle down, potentially in Berlin, until we're tired of that, too. You can see a more detailed itinerary.

In the next weeks, we plan to sell, store or pack all of our belongings. We have a list of items for sale, please take a look, and let us know and let us know if you're interested in purchasing any of these items.

If you would like to keep in touch and follow along with our journey, please come back to this website that we will use to post where we're going and other updates. Alternatively, FaceTime us!

This is truly a once in a lifetime opportunity!

We are unbelievably fortunate to have the means to be able to support ourselves regardless of the location at a time in our lives when we are not tied to any particular place. This opportunity couldn't have happened without the support of our friends, family, and colleagues, and we are so incredibly grateful for all that you have done for us.
