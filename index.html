In the final week of August, 2018, we announced that we will be leaving our home to go traveling.

After packing up our tiny apartment and selling our belongings, we took the once in a lifetime opportunity to see the world.

We are unbelievably fortunate to be able to support ourselves from any location at a time in our lives when our home consists only of easily movable people and objects. This opportunity couldn't have happened without the support of our friends, family, and colleagues.

We are so incredibly grateful for all that you have done for us!

Love,
